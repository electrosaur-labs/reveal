## Derived Works (PSD Files)

The 16-bit Lab PSD files in this repository are derived works created by converting source JPEG and TIFF images to Adobe Photoshop format using:

1. **JPEG to sRGB Decoding** - Standard JPEG decompression via Sharp.js
2. **sRGB to CIELAB Conversion** - Standard CIE 1976 L*a*b* color space conversion
3. **PSD Encoding** - 16-bit Lab PSD format with RLE compression

Since the source images are all CC0/Public Domain, the derived PSD files are also freely usable without restriction.
